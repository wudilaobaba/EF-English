补充：
How/What about+n/ving 或 should 建议句式的回答：
如：How about going to a movie?
肯定回答：
That's a great idea! 	这主意真棒！
That's a good idea.	好主意。
Cool!	棒极了！
OK, let's go!	好的，走吧！
否定回答：
I don't think so.	我不这么想。
That's a bad idea.	这主意不好。
No, thanks.	不了，谢谢。
Thanks, but I'm busy. / Thanks, but I'm not hungry.
