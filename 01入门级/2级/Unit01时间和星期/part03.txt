单词：
again 再次

短语：
arrive at...  到达某处
talk to colleagues 与同事交谈
have a meeting 开会
write a note 写便条
take a coffee break 休息一会
have a nice weekend 祝你有一个愉快的周末
this afternoon 今天下午

句子：
Could you tell me about your day at work? 能告诉我你的日常工作吗？
I arrive at the office at 8:30. 我八点半到办公室。
I have meetings. 我有会要开。
Can you come to a meeting? 你能来参加一个会吗？
Can we meet at 1 this afternoon?  今天下午一点我们能见面吗？
I'm not free at 1. 一点钟我没有空。
When are you free? 您什么时候有空呢？
Let's meet at 4 o'clock. 那我们四点见面吧。
Are you free on Sunday morning? 你周日早上有空吗？
I can only meet in the afternoon. 我只能下午见面
How's 2 pm ? 下午两点怎么样？
See you on Sunday at 2. 星期日下午2点见

补充：
用于表达请求的 Can 和 can't  这里不是询问能力哦
Can you do ...
1.Yse I can.
2.I can do ... at 10 o'clock.
3.若否定回答则使用：I'm sorry,but I can't.   I'm sorry,but I can't meet today.
