单词：✅
compliment 恭维、赞美 /ˈkɑːmplɪment/

短语：
right now 现在
take vitamins /teɪk ˈvaɪtəmɪns/ 服用维生素
smoke cigarettes /smoʊk ˈsɪɡərets/ 吸烟

句子：
You okay? 你还好吗？
I'm stressed out. 我筋疲力尽
My daughter's sick. 我女儿生病了
I'm sorry to hear that.听到这个消息我很抱歉
What's wrong with her?
She has the flu. 她得流感了。
How do you do it? 你是怎么做到的？(一般对方都要反问一句：Do what?)
You always look great. 你一直看上去身体很好
What's your secret? 您的秘诀是什么？
I eat well. 我吃的好(胃口好)
How do you stay in shape?	=== How do you stay healthy?你怎样保持身体健康的？
Are you exercising ?你在锻炼吗？
How are you doing? 你好吗？

A: You look great. What's your secret?您气色不错。有什么秘诀吗？
B: Thanks, but it's not a secret. I exercise, and I eat well.谢谢，没什么秘诀。我锻炼身体，胃口也好。




补充：
感官动词 look /feel /smell /taste /sound + adj  表示感官
You really look great!	您看起来气色真好！
Tom looks bored.
He looks stressed out. 他看起来筋疲力尽。
That cheese smells bad. 奶酪发臭了。
The music sounds good/beautiful/great.	音乐听起来很美。
He dosen't feel well. 他感觉不好。
A: Let's go to the movies.我们去看电影吧。
B: That sounds great/good! 听起来很棒！
