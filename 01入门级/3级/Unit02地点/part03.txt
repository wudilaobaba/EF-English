句子：
It's one block down B. 到B只有一个街区。
Which direction is it?
It's west.
Go down/alone Main Street for three blocks, and then make a right.	沿 Main Street 街走三个街区，然后右转。
Go south for two blocks, and turn left.	往南走两个街区，然后左转。
Where are you now? 你现在在哪？
Where are you coming from? 你从哪里来？
How are you coming?	你怎么来？
Thank you for your help! 谢谢您的帮忙！
How many desks do you have in your office? 你办公室有多少张桌子？

补充：具体描述地址
乘坐13路公交车，在Pine Street站下车，然后在第一大街向南走三个街区。
我们办公室在左边3489号。我们在4楼的420号办公室
Take the number 13 bus, and get off  at Pine Street.
Then walk north  on First Street for three blocks .
Our office is on the left  at 3489. We're on the fourth floor in office number 420.

补充：
如果打算拜访一家公司，你可能询问公司的地址和前去的路线。在问题前加上 please 显得更礼貌。
What's your address, please?
Where's your office, please?
How do I get to your office, please?
Can you please tell me how to get there?
