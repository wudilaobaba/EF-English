短语：
be good/okay/terrible/bad at doing/sth (不)擅长...
be interested in doing  对...感兴趣
I'm quite good at it.我相当擅长
listen to music 听音乐

句子：
I collect stamps.	我集邮。
I don't play them all day, like Simon does. 并不是每天都像Simon一样玩这些。
I have other interests.我有其他的兴趣。
I have lots of hobbies. 我有很多爱好

询问他人兴趣爱好的方法：
What are you interested in?	您对什么感兴趣？
What do you like doing?	您喜欢做什么？
What does she enjoy doing?
What's she into?  She's really into art.她很热衷于艺术。
What else do you like to do?

使用 belong to 表示你是某俱乐部的会员
We belong to a book club.	我们是书友会的会员。
使用 on 表示你是某运动队的队员。
They're on the running team.	他们是田径队的。
使用 get together with 谈论与朋友交往。
I like getting together with friends.	我喜欢跟朋友们聚在一起。
