单词：✅

短语：
Poor + 人名  可怜的xxx（表示同情）

句子：
Thanks for comming (to the party). 感谢参加
Thanks for inviting me. It's a great party.感谢邀请，派对很好
Thanks for having this party.谢谢举办这场派对。
How's your wife feeling? 你妻子还好吧？ ---对病人才使用feeling
She still has a cold. 他还是感冒着
Give her my best. 给他带个好
Thanks,I will.谢谢，我会的
//以下两句都以将来进行时的句式进行回答
So, what's happening with you? 那么，你近来怎么样？--Not much. / Not really 再加一句将来进行时的句子
What about you? Anything new? 你呢？近来怎样？
I'm taking some vacation time next week. 我下周要去度假
Thanks again for comming. 再次感谢参加
Love the music. What is it?	音乐真好听，是什么音乐？
Great snacks. / Love the snacks. Do you enjoy cooking?	点心很好吃。你喜欢做菜吗？
Who's your favorite singer? 你最喜欢的歌手是谁？
Wow! That sounds wonderful! 哇！听起来太好了!
Wow! That sounds incredible.哇！听起来不可思议。
I understand.我明白。

对话结束时使用以下作为结束：
Nice talking with/to you.	很高兴和你交谈。
Good to see you again.	很高兴再次见到你。
Say hello to Bella.	代我向 Bella 问好。

跟主人说要走了：
A: Well, it's late. I should go. 嗯，不早了，我该走了。
B: Thanks for coming.	谢谢光临。
A: Thanks again for the wonderful party.  派对很精彩，再次感谢。
B: You're welcome! Thanks for the wine.	别客气！谢谢你带的葡萄酒。

互相道别：
Bye! Have a good evening.再见！祝你晚上愉快。
B: See you soon.	再见。
